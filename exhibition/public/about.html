FEED THE BEAST

Feed the Beast is a living visualization of human connections.

Every point in this space represents a real person. Every line represents a real relationship. Together, they form a constantly evolving organism built from friendships, family ties, shared experiences, workplaces, schools, and chance encounters.

Most of these connections exist around us every day, but remain invisible. This project makes them visible.

As new people join and new relationships are added, the organism grows, shifts, and reorganizes itself. No one stands alone. Every person affects the structure of the whole network.

Explore the system to discover:

  • How people are connected to one another
  • Unexpected paths between strangers
  • Shared backgrounds, cities, workplaces, and schools
  • The role of weak ties in connecting distant parts of the network
  • How a single new connection can reshape the entire structure

HOW TO EXPLORE

Rotate Drag to move around the network. Zoom Pinch or scroll to move closer or further away. Select a Person Tap any node to open their profile and reveal their direct connections. Personal Network After selecting a person, tap SHOW NETWORK to hide the rest of the universe and focus entirely on their personal web of connections. Search Find people by name, profession, city, school, workplace, or other shared attributes. Path Finder Select two people to reveal the shortest chain of connections between them. Add Connection Join the network yourself and watch the organism adapt to your presence.

A LIVING NETWORK

The network is never complete.

Every new participant adds new pathways, new opportunities, and new ways of connecting people who may have never met.

Feed the Beast grows with every connection.
